# App Review Notes

TippyCam is a camera app. It uses the camera to take photos and Photos access to save and browse images in the in-app gallery.

No login is required. There are no purchases, subscriptions, ads, backend services, analytics, or third-party SDKs.

Review steps:

1. Launch the app.
2. Allow camera access.
3. Tap the shutter to take a photo.
4. Allow Photos access when prompted.
5. Open the bottom-left gallery thumbnail to view saved photos.
6. If available, switch to Portrait and take a portrait photo.
