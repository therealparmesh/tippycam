## Description

TippyCam is a simple iPhone camera for bright, vivid photos.

Open the app, frame the shot, and tap the shutter. TippyCam uses the iPhone camera pipeline, applies one on-device bright render, and saves the result to your Photos library. Portrait mode is available on supported devices.

No account, subscriptions, backend, ads, or cloud processing.
