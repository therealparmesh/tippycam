# TippyCam privacy policy

_Effective date: June 20, 2026_

TippyCam is designed to be private by default.

## What TippyCam uses

TippyCam uses the camera to take photos. It uses Photos access to save captured images and show your recent photos in the in-app gallery.

Captured photos are rendered locally on your device.

## Data Collection

TippyCam does not include accounts, advertising, analytics, backend services, cloud processing, or third-party SDKs.

Photos stay on your device unless you choose to share them through iOS or sync them with your own iCloud Photos settings. TippyCam does not send photos to a backend or cloud service.

## Permissions

- Camera is used to capture photos.
- Photos is used to save images and show your recent library items.

## Your choices

You can change Camera and Photos permissions in iPhone Settings. You can delete photos from the Photos app at any time.

## Children's privacy

TippyCam is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when TippyCam changes. The effective date above identifies the current version.
