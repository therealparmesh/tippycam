# TippyCam support

_Last updated: June 20, 2026_

TippyCam keeps camera capture simple: open the app, frame the shot, tap the shutter, and let the app save a bright rendered photo to your library.

## Getting Started

Allow Camera access when prompted. Allow Photos access so TippyCam can save captures and show your recent photos in the in-app gallery.

Use the camera flip button to switch between rear and front cameras. Use Photo for everyday shots and Portrait on supported devices.

## Photos

TippyCam saves rendered images to your Photos library. The in-app gallery shows recent photos from your library and opens the selected item using the system photo viewer.

## Privacy

TippyCam has no accounts, ads, analytics, backend, cloud processing, or third-party SDKs. Photos are captured, rendered, and saved on device.
